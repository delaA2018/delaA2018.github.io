Hey there! Are you tired of dissapointing food experiences? Say no more! We've created a food spot list that will surely get you excited. From mouth watering wings to a relaxing coffee, we got all the details just for you! Get ready to experience each place one at a time.

East Coast Wings + Grill

Location: 338 Tate St., Greensboro NC 27403
Specialty: Wings and Burgers
Hours of Operation:
Mon - Fri : 11am - 9pm
Sat & Sun : 12pm - 9pm

Bacon Cheddar Burger

$12.00

The Bacon Cheddar Burger has 2 patties with Applewood smoked bacon, cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, and mayo. It is a tasty burger that will leave you satisfied.

Bone-In Wing

$5.00

The 5 Bone-In Wing has a Heat Index (Virgin, Dry Rub, Mild, Medium, Hot, X-Hot, Volcanic, Insanity), dressing option (none, ranch, or bleu cheese) , and side option (none, Celery, or Carrots)

Southwestern Chicken Wrap

$10.99

The Southwestern Chicken Wrap comes with chipotle glazed grilled chicken with shredded lettuce, tomatoes, cheddar, mozzarella cheese and ranch. You can also choose a side (chips, tots, fries, or celery/carrots).

Gong Cha

Location: 334 Tate St., Greensboro NC 27403
Specialty: Bubble Tea
Hours of Operation:
Mon - Sat : 11am - 8pm
Sun : Closed

Brown Sugar Milk Tea

$5.00

This drink is Black milk tea with brown sugar. You can also add more toppings for additional charge. It is a refreshing drink to take on the go to class.

Matcha Tea Latte

$5.25

This beverage will be loved by all Matcha beverage lovers. It uses a japanese style green tea with milk. I enjoy adding add. topping such as the boba.

Slushie Taro Milk

$5.00

This Taro Milk drink stands out from the rest because it comes in the form of a slushie. While it is known for its sweet nutty flavor, it can be good for the gut since it is considered rich in fiber.

Tate Street Coffee House

Location: 334 Tate St, Greensboro NC 27403
Specialty: Coffe, Pastries, and Sandwiches
Hours of Operation:
Mon - Fri : 6:30am - 6pm
Sat & Sun : 7am - 6pm

Blueberry Muffin

$2.50

These freshly made pastries are a wonderful snack! The muffin also has sugar on top. This snack can be enjoyed almost anywhere and a good recharge during those long study sessions.

Esspresso Drink

$5.50

If you're looking bold flavors then an Espresso will be just right for you. The drink also comes in a decorative coffee mug. You can also add cream and sugar packets found at the tables.

Peppermint Hot Chocolate

$6.00

The peppermint syrup upgrades the hot chocolate to have a minty and cool taste. No matter how cold it is outside, the hot beverage will be sure to warm you up. Sadly, they have not added marshmallows as an add. topping option for now.
